Privacy

Policy.

At JobsPortal, we value the privacy of our users. This policy outlines how we handle the limited data collected on our platform.

1. Data Collection (Recruiters)

When you submit a job listing, we collect your Company Name, Email Address, and WhatsApp Number. This data is required to allow job seekers to contact you. Once a job is approved, this information is made public on our website.

2. Data Collection (Job Seekers)

We do not collect personal data from job seekers. We do not require registration, accounts, or CV uploads to our server. Your applications go directly to the recruiter via your own email client or WhatsApp application.

3. Cookies & Tracking

JobsPortal is a lightweight website. We do not use tracking cookies or analytics to monitor your personal browsing habits. We value a clean, private experience.

4. Data Storage

All job data is stored in a secure file on our server. We do not sell, trade, or rent recruiter contact information to third-party marketing companies.

5. Data Removal

Recruiters can request the removal of their job listing and contact details at any time by contacting us at raf@gmail.com. Once a job reaches its deadline, it may be manually removed from our public listings.

6. External Links

Our site contains links to WhatsApp and Email services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external platforms.
